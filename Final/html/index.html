Welcome to our site

The current situation of the market leads an intelligent shopper to question if the item that they are buying is actually made of the right kind of material, the material that they are actually paying for. Hence, the old general problem of tracking the upstream and downstream movement of goods and payments is addressed through this project. We try to resolve these issues by shifting the already existing supply chains over the blockchain platform to institute trust on both sides and instill fair trade
